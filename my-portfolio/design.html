CAITLIN KUAN

DESIGN

PORTFOLIO

GLASS HALF-FULL;

An animation and digital design project exploring the reality of depression in an dystopian, cyber-punk world. This animation was made as part of a Digital Internal and Art Design portfolio over 2-3 months.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Blender, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Animate

Awards: Top Art Exhibition 2023, Outstanding Art Scholarship (NCEA), Technology Scholarship

Life comes to a standstill, locked out as the feeling of emptiness becomes overwhelming. Until a small robot is found in a dumpster. From there, life seems to take a good turn. New hope gets set alight as the world can be seen with new eyes.
